About isl-Tech

Our story, our mission, and our commitment to Pacific tech innovation.

🇸🇧 Built in the Solomon Islands, for the world

isl-Tech was founded with a simple belief: the Solomon Islands deserves world-class technology that works for our unique context. We're not just importing foreign solutions — we're building our own.

From Honiara to the remote provinces, our tools are designed to work with limited bandwidth, offline-capable where it matters, and priced for the local economy. Yet they're built to standards that compete anywhere in the world.

Whether you're a small business owner in Gizo, a student in Auki, or a developer in Sydney — isl-Tech has something for you. We specialize in mobile apps, web platforms, and digital products that bridge the gap between Pacific needs and global technology.

🎯 Our Mission

To accelerate digital transformation across the Solomon Islands by building accessible, reliable, and locally-relevant technology — while showcasing Pacific innovation to the rest of the world.

👨‍💻 About the Founder

isl-Tech is a solo venture — one developer passionate about using code to solve real problems for Pacific Island communities. From building your first business website to launching cross-platform mobile apps, every project gets personal attention and a deep understanding of local context.
